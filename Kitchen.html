Kitchen Services

The Kitchen page invites you to indulge in a culinary experience tailored to your preferences. Our thoughtfully curated menu is categorized into Starter, Main Course, Dessert, and Beverages, providing a comprehensive selection to satisfy your palate. Navigate through these delectable offerings, where you have the flexibility to choose the quantity of each item according to your event's needs. As you make your culinary selections, please be informed that the billing for these items will be generated separately. Upon finalizing your choices, a detailed email with the itemized billing will be sent to you. We understand the importance of confirming your purchase, and as such, you will have the opportunity to review the billing information and confirm your order via email.
